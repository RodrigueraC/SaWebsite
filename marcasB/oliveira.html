Oliveira
Foundation

Oliveira Foundation is a NGO that provides support to others NGOs. Oliveira means Olive tree, and that relates to their mission: see the NGOs and even inside projects as branches of a biggest tree. The logo type already existed, the major problem was the old icon and how to visually organize all the brands inside Oliveira Foundation.

  • Português
