Transformando
As Nações

This huge project is a partnership between John Maxwell Foundation and Oliveira Foundation. Their major goal is to teach kids, teenagers and young adults how to handle emotional questions, familie issues, friends and a future career.

  • English
  • Português

    • The challenge here was to create an identity that gives a large support for a range of materials: social media, books, letters. I choose two colors that work well in all these cases and help create a strong identity, easy to recognize. Also thought about simple icones that can help me bring life to the piece, being more playful or just filling the space.

    The type is very simple, as long as the brand communicates to children and young adults, and the icon suggests moving forward.
