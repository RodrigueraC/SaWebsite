Carina
Diniz

Carina Diniz is a brand that handles people.
She is out there looking for talents, recruiting people and connecting those professionals with amazings opportunities.

  • English
  • Português

    • For me, the biggest difference between other brands, is the way that Carina sees her work. For her, the job is actually see the whole context and made this connections, understanding what the company needs and what is the talent's habilities.

    She has the ability of treating people with kindness, see the situation in a light way and planning the solution with her technical skills.

    As we spoke before, the key of her work is how she see people, so I worked with the concept of lenz. They make you see better, analyzing things and correct an eventual problem. So the idea was transforming the letters CDG into lenz.

    Also I choose a serif font, to connect with the values of commitment, results, authority and exclusivity. And finality, for being more playful, I thought that will be cool if her signature works as a raised power, being in the superior part of the logo. The message here is that what she dos is her super power, and elevates her at that power.
