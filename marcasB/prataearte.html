Prata
E Arte

"Mais prata no seu bronze" was the summer 2021/2022 campaign for the Prata e Arte. They needed a campaign that was able to survive through all the summer and was still related to the carnaval as well. Also was necessary a series of key visuals that worked with models' photographs, products and types.

  • English
  • Português

    • My first step was to focus on a major color palette for the campaign, and pick secundaries colors for one of the special occasions: carnival and beach. I tried too, related little symbols to each situation, that I used as anchors for the layout.

    With those colors, I created some backgrounds that match with the vibe campaign: very warm, golden and beachy. For the photos, the instruction for the photographer was hard shadows on the products and golden light on the models.

    In the beginning, this campaign would be only on social media and digital environment, but worked so well that the brand decided to transform into something physical into the stores too.
