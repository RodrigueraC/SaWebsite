Capuchinha

Capuchinha is a restaurant specializing in Brazilian cuisine. They look to the north and north east of the country to create a unique, delicious and original menu. All the experience was created to immerse the customers and teach them about local producers, seasonal ingredients and ancestral gastronomy. The Capuchinha's mission is to create a taste's repertoire, contributing to the culture.

    • Capuchinha is a charming flower and as the brand's name, represents the icon as well. I called an artist friend and asked him for some sketches of the flower. I wanted some fluid, organic and with heavy lines. He sent me some options, and I worked on it until the final shape to the icon.

    This little species used to grow like chains through the walls or soil, and I thought about it for the type's shape. I tried mixing them, one lead to another, creating some beautiful movement between the letters.

    For the colors, they came from the flower's ones, but with some adjustments The original tones were bright and warm, and in the final palette, they are still warm, but in a more soft way.
