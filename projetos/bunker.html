Bunker dos
Bigodes

Bunker dos Bigodes is a NGO that helps cats with Felv to be adopted. Felv is an illness that complicated cats lifes, but is totally possible to live with, and also complicated the search for a new owner. Knowing that challenge, the NGO creates every year a calendar, to promote the cats and to get donations.

  • English
  • Português

    • I made the 2021 calendar, and I liked to imagine this calendar as an opportunity to introduce all the cats in a fancy and fun way. So my plan was draw bow ties in the male cats and bows in the female cats, as the ditinction of sex is important in this context.

    I created a fancy frame for their photograph and chose their best looks (which was no big deal at all, because they all are SO cute). For the first time I put the cat's name on it too, so if someone falls in love with this cat, already knows its name and can get in touch with the NGO asking for their match.
