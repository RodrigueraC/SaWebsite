Sabá

Sabá is my personal project, where I can create small projects trying to interpret literature through design. The concept is always thinking about the text's meaning and how I can express this meaning in an aesthetic way. I also like to combine other areas, like dance, photography, and art, which gives me the opportunity to collaborate with friends and create something meaningful for me.

  • English
  • Português

    • I used a lot of women's works as references to this brand, especially Frieda Harris. As the name is Sabá, she was perfect for the context, combined with the witchcraft universe.

    My principal idea was to create a symbol that synthesizes this concept and the name, which I did working on the letter a.
