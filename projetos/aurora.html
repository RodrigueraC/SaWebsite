Aurora

Aurora is a NGO that fights for human rights. In the beginning of the pandemic, they gave me the opportunity to work on this little editorial project, a book club for disgusting human rights.

  • English
  • Português

    • The book club already existed before the pandemic, but in 2020 was the first time that it worked only by video calls. So this document helps those people evolve how to organize this situation and make it work in the best way possible.

    The first pages are instructions and then every page is a different book suggestion, talking about possibilities and themes to the group argue about.
